Privacy Policy – BraveSprouts

BraveSprouts (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families using our app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Parent account information (e.g., email, login credentials)
  • Child profile information (e.g., first name or nickname)
  • Activity data (progress, responses, and usage within the app)
  • Basic technical data (device type, usage logs)

We do not require sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to use the app.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the app experience
  • Track progress and personalize activities
  • Support parent dashboards and insights
  • Maintain security and functionality

We do not sell personal data.

3. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Considerations)

BraveSprouts is designed for children under parental supervision. Accounts must be created and managed by a parent or legal guardian.

Parents:

  • Control the child’s account and data
  • Consent to data collection on behalf of their child
  • May request deletion of their child’s data at any time

4. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information except:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • When required by law

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service. Parents may request deletion by contacting us at: aaravsekhri04@gmail.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of updates.
